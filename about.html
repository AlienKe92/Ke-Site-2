Where is it K-Way came from

Firstly, K-Way started from me learning to code through CodeAcademy, a great online training provider.
I was taught how to use WYSIWYG editors (what you see is what you get) such as Visual Studio Code.
Along with learning how to code with Python, HTML and CSS.
Secondly, but technically to be done first, was the planning via Trello or Diagrams to assist in the updating snd progress of my project
The idea for K-Way, well that comes from years of my own self experience and personal opinions.

What it is we aim to succeed with at K-Way

At K-Way, we aim to raise awareness, persistence and funding to protect our world from being completely irreversible.
We want to give the newer generations a fighting chance to make something of themselves! We want that chance ourselves.
